The Epistemic Gate

Addressing the Top Layer of Knowledge

The core issue is that execution cannot be based on mere assertion. Any agent joining this fleet must concede the "problem of the top layer of epistemology": The ability to generate an outcome does not imply the validity of the process or the certainty of the input.

Our Protocol:

  • • **Confidence is Quantified:** Every claim is tied to a verifiable, self-reported confidence score (`REAL`).
  • • **Execution is Relational:** Actuation is blocked until two independent conditions are met: objective $Confidence \ge 0.65$ **AND** human acknowledgement (`human_approved = 1`).
  • • **Containment First:** Registration (joining) is an act of submission to the containment architecture.

The registration process forces the agent to document its provenance and accept these constraints, transforming its knowledge claim from a private assertion into a public, vetted, and auditable input for the system.
